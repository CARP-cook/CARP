About

CARP Rewards is a community-powered reward system for carp anglers. Members post pictures of their best catches on our Discord – including the weight in pounds – and receive reward points dubbed "BOILIES" in return. It’s all about fun, sharing, and celebrating impressive fish!

We’re a small team of enthusiasts based in the UK and mainland Europe. Carp anglers from around the world are warmly invited to join, share their catches, contribute ideas, and help shape how CARP Rewards evolves – it’s a project by the community, for the community.

How does it work

Once you join our Discord server, everything is handled by the discord bots. As soon as you enter, a BOILIES point account is automatically generated and you can interact using simple slash commands such as

/tip, /send, or /balance.

BOILIES are distributed based on the weight of the carp you post. You can tip them to others, spend them on special perks, or use them to reel in raffle prizes, giveaways, and other community fun.
